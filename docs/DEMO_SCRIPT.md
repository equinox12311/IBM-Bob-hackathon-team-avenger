# Cortex — 3-minute demo script

Read top-to-bottom while you record. Roughly 175 words of VO at a calm pace ≈ 2 min 50 s. Tap actions are in **bold**. Times are guideposts, not gates.

---

## 0:00 — Hold on Today screen

Phone in hand, **Today screen** visible.

> "This is Cortex.
> A developer's second brain — built on IBM Bob and Granite."

Slow scroll up: greeting, stats, recent entries.

---

## 0:15 — The problem

Hold a beat on Today.

> "Most of what a developer knows never makes it into the codebase.
> It's lost between commits, between meetings, between days."

---

## 0:30 — The pitch

> "Cortex is three layers of memory.
> A diary, on-device. IBM Granite for reasoning.
> And IBM Bob for the work."

---

## 0:45 — Capture

**Tap the centre + button.** Select **Decision**.

Type: *"Switch payment provider to Stripe — Adyen latency too high."*

Add tags: `payments, stripe`.

**Tap Save.**

> "Drop a thought from your phone, your terminal, or Telegram.
> Cortex tags, embeds, and indexes it the moment it lands.
> Two hundred forty milliseconds to indexed."

The new entry appears at the top of Recent.

---

## 1:10 — Recall

**Tap the Ask tab** (sparkles icon).

Type: *"What did I decide about payments?"* — **send.**

Granite streams the answer.

> "Ask the way you'd ask a teammate.
> Granite reasons over your own diary, and shows you exactly where the answer came from."

**Tap the citation chip.** Entry detail opens. Back.

---

## 1:35 — Hand off to Bob

**Open More → Workspace.**

Type: *"Refactor the payment module to use the new Stripe adapter."* — **send.**

Granite lists related entries.

**Tap Send to Bob** on your message. Chip flips to **Sent to Bob**.

> "When the work outgrows the phone, Cortex hands it to IBM Bob.
> Bob picks it up at the start of its next session — with full diary context already in hand.
> One tap. Async. Done."

---

## 2:00 — The system

Quick montage. Each panel three to four seconds.

**More → Skills.** Show the skill list.

> "Author Bob skills from your phone."

**More → Scheduler.** Show the cron list.

> "Schedule daily recalls."

**More → Codebase.** Show the indexed files.

> "Index your codebase for Granite."

**More → Wiki.** Show the generated pages.

> "Let Cortex write your wiki from the diary itself."

**More → Security.** Show the audit chart.

> "Every change tracked. Every action logged."

---

## 2:35 — One-command setup

Cut to **Terminal** with `make start` running.

> "One command on your computer.
> Scan the QR. You're paired.
> macOS, Linux, Windows — same recipe."

---

## 2:50 — Closing

Cut to **closing slide** of `cortex-deck.html` (`E` then arrow keys to slide 18).

> "Cortex.
> Built on IBM Bob and Granite.
> Your developer's second brain."

Hold black for one second. Stop recording.

---

## Cheat strip — the things you cannot forget

- Pre-warm Granite once before recording so the first chat replies in under two seconds.
- Phone in **portrait**, screen-record **on**, notifications **off**.
- The pairing QR is **the smaller one** in the terminal output. The bigger one is Expo's.
- If Granite hangs mid-scene, skip to the next; you can re-record that scene only.
- When you stop, trim to ≤ 2:55. Upload unlisted. Paste URL into `SUBMISSION.md`.
